ANNALENA'S - CANADA'S BEST NEW RESTURANT

Posted on July 17th, 2016
Located in Kitsilano, AnnaLena was named best new resturant in 2015 by En Route Air Canada. The atmosphere here is great, food was delicious and the staff was very friendly and knowledgable. It is usually very busy here so I do recommend making reservations a week prior to coming here!
